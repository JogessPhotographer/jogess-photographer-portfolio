Cars

Air Mauritius operates the ATR 72, specifically the -500 and -600 series, for regional and short-haul routes within the Indian Ocean region. These turboprop aircraft are known for their reliability, fuel efficiency, and ability to operate from airports with short runways like Plaine Corail in Rodrigues. Air Mauritius utilizes the ATR 72 to connect travelers to neighboring islands and underserved destinations.

The Emirates Airbus A380-800 is a luxurious, double-deck, wide-body aircraft known for its spacious cabins and premium amenities. Emirates' A380s feature unique offerings like Shower Spas in First Class and an Onboard Lounge for Business Class passengers. The aircraft also boasts updated interiors with cream and bronze tones, textured waves, and the UAE national tree motif.
